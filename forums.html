Forums

The Magnus Institute is an academic organisation centred on research into the esoteric and paranormal. We are constantly looking for ways to make our extensive catalogue available to the public and extending and broadening how we gather our research.
Our staff are currently working towards fetching us online, both with our Institute website and an extensive rollout of new technology to all departments thanks to a generous benefactor.

We are happy to announce the rollout of our new interactive Research Forums. We hope they will become a useful tool for both staff and enquiring minds, in the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment.

The Forums are currently under construction; we hope to have them available for all as soon as possible. Over the coming months, they will open up the Institute's online doors to the curious and the esoteric-minded.
