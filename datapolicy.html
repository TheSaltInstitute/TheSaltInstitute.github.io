Data Collection Policy

Main website

We do not collect any data beyond what you yourself submit via forms or by sending an email to addresses displayed on the website.

The data we receive will be used as follows:

  • The Online Statement and Guest Book forms are broken on purpose. You can enter anything you want into the fields; clicking "Submit" will not send any data to our servers at all.
  • The Contact form will collect the data you enter and create an email with it using your preferred email software on your device. If you do not send the resulting email, no data will be collected. If you do send the resulting email, an automated response will be sent to your email address; we do not keep your email address beyond that and we will not use it for any other purposes. We will not collect any other data.
  • If you send an email to mail@themagnusinstitute.org or tech@themagnusinstitute.org, an automated response will be sent to your email address. We do not keep your email address beyond that and we will not use it for any other purposes.
